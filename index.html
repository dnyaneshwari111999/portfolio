Tic Tac Toe

Tic-tac-toe, also known as noughts and crosses, is a simple two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns placing their marks (X or O) in empty squares, with the goal of getting three in a row (horizontally, vertically , or diagonally) to win. If the board is filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw
